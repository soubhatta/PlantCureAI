Why PlantCure AI

Moto and Objective Behind PlantCure AI

PlantCure AI aims to revolutionize plant disease detection through advanced artificial intelligence. Our commitment is to empower farmers, gardeners, and plant enthusiasts with tools for early disease detection, contributing to sustainable and healthy agriculture practices across India and throughout the whole worldwide.

Our mission is driven by the need to combat plant diseases that threaten food security, and we envision a future where AI and agriculture unite to safeguard global crops and resources.

Technologies used to Build the Model

  • Front End & UI Technologies – HTML, CSS, Javascript
  • Back End Development – Python, Flask, Gemini API
  • Deep Learning Frameworks – Tensorflow, Keras, CNN
  • Machine Learning Libraries – Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib
  • Computer Vision AI Based Models – Open CV, Pillow
  • IDE Tools & Platforms – Visual Studio Code, Google Colab
  • Version Tools & Deployment – Git, Github, Vercel, Render
